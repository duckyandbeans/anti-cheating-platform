English Proficiency Assessment Portal for ESL Teacher Candidates

Assessment Instructions

  • The assessment consists of two components: a 100-question English Proficiency Test and a Speaking Test.
  • Ensure that you have a stable internet connection before beginning.
  • Please mute all notifications on your devices to avoid interruptions during the assessments.
  • Complete the assessments independently without assistance from other people or external resources.
  • During the English Proficiency Test, candidates should remain on the examination tab. The testing platform may record instances of leaving the exam page.
  • You may complete the English Proficiency Test in your current room or location.
  • Once you have completed the proficiency test and are ready to begin the Speaking Test, please move to another room or any quiet space available to you to ensure that your recording is clear and free from background noise and distractions.
  • Allocate sufficient uninterrupted time to complete both components of the assessment.
  • After moving to a quiet environment, proceed to the Speaking Test and follow the recording instructions provided.
