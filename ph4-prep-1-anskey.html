Part 3: Impersonal Language

1. People say that the children are afraid of ghosts.
It is said that the children are afraid of ghosts.
The children are said to be afraid of ghosts.
2. They suppose that this book will become a best seller.
It is supposed that this book will become a best seller.
This book is supposed to become a best seller.
3. People say that men drive better than women.
It is said that men drive better than women.
Men are said to drive better than women.
4. Some people believe that the world will end soon.
It is believed that the world will end soon.
The world is believed to end soon.
5. Everyone thinks that an engine failure was the cause of my car accident.
It is thought that an engine failure was the cause of my car accident.
An engine failure is thought to have been the cause of my car accident.
6. Her neighbors suppose that she moved here at the age of 25.
It is supposed that she moved here at the age of 25.
She is supposed to have moved here at the age of 25.
7. The police think that this man had robbed a bank.
It is thought that this man had robbed a bank.
This man is thought to have robbed a bank.
8. Many scientists believe that some planets are inhabited.
It is believed that some planets are inhabited.
Some planets are believed to be inhabited.
9. His friend said that Peter was the strongest boy in the class.
It was said that Peter was the strongest boy in the class.
Peter was said to have been the strongest boy in the class.
10. People say that the corona virus came from China.
It is said that the corona virus came from China.
The corona virus is said to have come from China.
11. People know that she is a good swimmer.
It is known that she is a good swimmer.
She is known to be a good swimmer.
12. They say that Francis is in hospital.
It is said that Francis is in hospital.
Francis is said to be in hospital.
13. They think that the children are in bed.
It is thought that the children are in bed.
The children are thought to be in bed.
14. People believe that the robber has worked in the bank.
It is believed that the robber has worked in the bank.
The robber is believed to have worked in the bank.
15. People believe that nuclear power stations are dangerous.
It is believed that nuclear power stations are dangerous.
Nuclear power stations are believed to be dangerous.
16. His colleagues thought that he was on holiday.
It was thought that he was on holiday.
He was thought to be on holiday. (or 'to have been on holiday')
17. People know that cars pollute the environment.
It is known that cars pollute the environment.
Cars are known to pollute the environment.
18. They suppose that the new product will come out soon.
It is supposed that the new product will come out soon.
The new product is supposed to come out soon.
19. They believe that she will win a gold medal.
It is believed that she will win a gold medal.
She is believed to win a gold medal.

Part 4: Reported Speech

Exercise 1: Punctuation

  • 1. John said, "I am very happy to meet you."
  • 2. The doctor said, "You should cut down on your salt intake."
  • 3. Natasha explained, "I am here to learn, not to fool around."
  • 4. "Pack your bags and get out of here!" screamed the landlord.
  • 5. "I believe," Toshiko said, "that you are holding my bag."
  • 6. "Can you tell me how to get to Route 17?" Atsuko asked.
  • 7. "The fastest way out is through the library," Robert explained.
  • 8. The professor said, "We will have a test on Friday."
  • 9. Boris yelled, "Where are my new shoes?"
  • 10. "I think that you are wrong," Oleg stated, "and I also feel that you don't understand the subject at all."

  • 9. Lisa said that Juan would never do it on time.
